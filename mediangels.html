At CanceRX Foundation, we help cancer patients with financial access to treatment. We specially focus on early stage patient cases or cases where there is no visible symptoms of disease who are ignored. We strongly feel that these patients are deserving and can get cured and benefit the most with the help of fundraisers. To take care of this pain point, we have formulated a " Medi-Angels Miracle Club", a group of sliked minded people with a philanthropic bent of mind and who strongly believe in giving back to the society by contribute regularly to help us a build a corpus of funds. This corpus of funds helps patients with finances for their cancer treatment irrespective of stage, visible symptoms.

How does it work?

Time is the essence of our initiative. Any treatment if not initiated on priority can be life threatening for the patient. Hence our exclusive members commit regular donations on a monthly basis for us to focus on our objective to ensure that we extend our help to all deserving patients without any delay in a continuous manner on a long term basis. This predictable source of income further assists us on mobilising activities related to our cause of providing the best treatment to the underprivileged.

About Us


MediAngels contribute and help us with a corpus of funds to be used to help cancer patients. We help with 80G benefits.


We check and validate genuineness of queries, disburse funds for treatment of patients as required


At the end of the year, we share utilisation of the corpus upon request with our medi angels
